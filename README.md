# Replay Driver Helper

A simple, CSP‑powered utility that improves navigation during Assetto Corsa replays. Replay Driver Helper automatically shows a compact driver list window whenever you enter replay mode, allowing you to instantly switch focus to any connected driver with a single click.
Perfect for content creators, league broadcasters, and anyone who wants smoother replay control.

The app appears automatically when a replay starts and hides itself when the replay ends.

Scroll through all connected drivers and click any name to immediately focus their car.
