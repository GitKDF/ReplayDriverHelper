# Replay Driver Helper

## About
Do you run online races and ever want to watch the replay later?  Have you wanted to switch to
another driver to see their perspective and hate that you have to scroll through the drivers
by number until you find the right car, or that you have no idea who you're looking at when
you switch?  Then this mod is for you!

Replay Driver Helper is a simple, CSP‑powered utility that automatically shows a compact
driver list window whenever you enter replay mode, allowing you to instantly switch focus
to any connected driver with a single click.

After installing, select All Apps at the top of the right-side-screen-menu, and activate
Replay Driver Helper.  The app appears automatically when a replay starts but remains
hidden in driving mode.

Changing cars with the default car selector box on the replay UI will change the
highlighted driver.  Click on any Driver's name to immediately switch focus their car.

## Limitations
The car number on the replay UI unfortunately doesn't update when the driver is changed
by clicking in the list.
